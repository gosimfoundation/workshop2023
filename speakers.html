With decades of research and work experience in graphics and animation, outside of his professional duties, he also served as the Industrial Chair for Eurographics, the Industrial Chair for the Encyclopedia of Computer Graphics and Games, and the Senior Associate Editor for the ACM Journal on Entertainment Computing. 2015 Dr. Wu Xiaomao founded ParticleTech and ventured into the third-generation proprietary graphics engine, GritGene.
